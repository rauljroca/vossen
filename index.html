We are Real Estate Developers

As developers, we transform lands, plots, or buildings in high value assets.

More than 20 years of experience gave us the ability to master all development phases, keeping quality, cost, and time under control through an extensive knowledge in development, project management and risk analysis and assessment.

We divide the development process into a first pre-development phase which includes the asset research, the development of the feasibility study, the project finance, and the acquisition process. Afterwards, in the construction phase we develop the project conception, the design and planning, we undertake the management of the construction and the project handover. Finally, the post-development phase where we manage the marketing and the selling or operation of the building.

We are Project Managers

We started being project managers. The successful Project Management service is the one which meets employer’s requirements and after been managing international projects for more than 20 years, we really know how to do it. Our experience comes from projects of different nature such as residential, hospitality, industrial, retail and infrastructure.

We set and manage teams, procurement, cost, time, risks, and stakeholders, we can assure that thanks to our extensive and deep knowledge of the industry, we can steer construction projects into success.

We are aligned with the most demanding international standards as PMI@, ISO 21500 and RICS.
